# Product Requirements: Minimap x Fandom Hybrid This app combines the social gaming tracking of Minimap with the deep community-driven content of Fandom. ## Core Features 1. **Game Tracking & Library:** Log play status (playing, completed, dropped), track hours, and manage your collection. 2. **Wiki Integration:** Seamless access to Fandom's deep wiki content directly from game pages. 3. **Community Feed:** Social feed featuring reviews, guides, and discussions from the Fandom community. 4. **Gaming News:** Integration with Fandom's editorial and news content. 5. **User Profiles:** Showcase gaming stats, library, and community contributions. ## Proposed Screens - **Dashboard/Home:** A personalized feed featuring current game progress, trending Fandom news, and community highlights. - **Game Detail (Minimap style):** Overview of a specific game, including tracking controls, rating, and quick links to the Fandom wiki. - **Fandom Wiki Section (Integrated):** A streamlined view of game-specific wiki content (e.g., characters, items, walkthroughs). - **Game Library:** A grid or list view of the user's collection, categorized by play status. - **Community/Explore:** A hub for finding new games and popular Fandom communities.
